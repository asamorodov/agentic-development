# Workflow stages

## Stage 0 — Inventory

Confirm all inputs exist. Do not rewrite.

## Stage 1 — Skeleton

Create `current/Theoretical_synthesis_rebuilt.md` with approved top-level structure only.

## Stage 2 — Dossiers

Create or update deep dossiers for:

- AI-SDLC frame;
- SWE-chat / Programming by Chat;
- SPDD;
- Spec Kit;
- Kiro;
- TDAD;
- Constitutional SDD;
- Gas Town / Beads;
- evidence/review/benchmark cluster;
- governance/open-source policy cluster;
- lifecycle tail/debt/security cluster.

## Stage 3 — Part briefs

For each part: governing thesis, lifecycle function, cases, source gaps, risks.

## Stage 4 — Drafting batches

Draft in approved batches, not whole document at once.

## Stage 5 — Audits

Anti-catalog, source-depth, anti-degradation where applicable.

## Stage 6 — Repair

Repair only with targeted changes; avoid uncontrolled expansion.

## Stage 7 — Human gate

Submit decision report.
