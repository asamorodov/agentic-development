# Anti-degradation audit for SPDD and Gas Town

Run after any SPDD or Gas Town rewrite.

Inputs:

- old baseline seed section;
- rebuilt section;
- source additions;
- old and new headings.

Check:

1. All old headings mapped or explicitly justified.
2. All named mechanisms preserved.
3. All concrete workflow details preserved or deepened.
4. No source-specific detail removed silently.
5. No old dense explanation replaced by a generic summary.
6. New AI-driven SDLC framing added without flattening old content.
7. Links and source references preserved or improved.
8. Any removed material listed under “Requires human gate.”

Output:

- `reports/SPDD_ANTI_DEGRADATION_REPORT.md`
- `reports/GAS_TOWN_ANTI_DEGRADATION_REPORT.md`
