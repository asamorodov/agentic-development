# Search step 1 results — SBOM official/standard layer

## Selected sources

- CISA/NTIA SBOM minimum elements — data fields, automation support, practices/processes.
- SPDX — ISO/IEC 5962:2021 standard, important for security/license compliance and supply-chain artifacts.
- CycloneDX — widely used SBOM standard, also expanding into CBOM and other bill-of-materials forms.
- SBOM transparency sources — SBOMs support vulnerability/license analysis and risk management.

## Evaluation

SBOM is a first-class lifecycle artifact for dependencies and supply chain. It belongs to Part XII and security/provenance, not only security appendix.
