# Search step 3 results — decision provenance / adjacent concepts

## Selected sources

- Design rationale literature — emphasizes reasons, alternatives, trade-offs and argumentation behind design.
- Architectural decision management / knowledge management — supports decision backlog, decision ready/done concepts, and decision sharing.
- RFC/IETF model — useful analogy for immutable/public historical record and proposal-to-standard maturation.
- LLM ADR violation detection — becomes important here again: decision records can become machine-checkable constraints only when they are explicit enough.

## Evaluation

The broader term is not “ADR” but **decision provenance**. ADR is one concrete file shape. RFC/design proposal, decision backlog and design rationale are other shapes.

## Impact on theory

Use “decision provenance” as the main concept. ADR is the practical artifact. This avoids overfitting to one template and makes it fit AI-driven SDLC.
